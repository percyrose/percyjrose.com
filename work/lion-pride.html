Lion PRIDE Caucus

The Lion PRIDE Caucus is
the umbrella LGBTQIA+ student organization at the Pennsylvania State University.  Through community organization, event coordination, information distribution, and holistic education, Lion PRIDE Caucus aims towards the continual construction of an increasingly open and inclusive community at Penn State University Park.

The Queer Leadership Council (QLC) is a group overseen by the Lion PRIDE Caucus, consisting of student leaders of the various LGBTQ+ student organizations at Penn State Main Campus. It exists to ensure that the various organizations remain connected with one another.
