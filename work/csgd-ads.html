CSGD Event Advertisements

The Jeffrey A. Conrad Center for Sexual and Gender Diversity (CSGD) is a resource center for LGBTQIA+ students at Penn State University. Throughout the spring, fall, and summer semesters, the CSGD hosts both educational and social events with the goals of sharing resources, cultivating a community, and teaching about various LGBTQ+ subjects and identities. All events have advertisements created for them, with variants for both digital and print signage specs as well as for social media formats.
