Keynote Speaker Cory Wade

Cory Wade is an LGBTQ+ activist and spokesperson. He is best known as the first openly gay man to participate on America's Next Top Model. As the Keynote Speaker of Penn State Pride Month 2023, Cory came to campus and spoke to students about LGBTQ+ identities, his current advocacy work, and his own lived experiences.
